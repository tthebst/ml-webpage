DeepSpeech

This is the deepspeech model from mozilla. It can translate speech to text. It uses memory units like LSTM's to transcribe the input. The deepspeech was revisited over time, but the initial version was released in 2015. If you notice bad translations, it is due to skew towards their training set. These are research model and not made real-world usage.
